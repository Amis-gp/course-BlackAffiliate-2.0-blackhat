# New Method for Bypassing Creative Moderation

## Overview

This advanced technique allows you to bypass Facebook's creative moderation system by leveraging the platform's language targeting feature. By strategically distributing "white" and "black" creatives across different language settings, you can significantly increase your approval rates.

Scroll down to locate the **"Languages"** section and activate it.

### Step 4: Strategic Language Selection

Here's the core of the method:

1. **Primary Language (Decoy)**
   - Select a language that is **NOT** your target language
   - Examples: Afrikaans, Arabic, Swahili, etc.
   - Purpose: To distract the moderation system

2. **Secondary Language (Your Target)**
   - Add German (or your actual target language)
   - Upload your **"black creative"** here
   - This is what your actual audience will see

3. **Remaining Languages (Safety Net)**
   - Upload your **"white creative"** for all other languages
   - These are compliant creatives that won't trigger moderation
   - Purpose: To dilute the algorithm's attention

## How It Works

### The Psychology Behind the Method

The Facebook moderation system scans multiple elements of your ad. By providing numerous compliant "white" creatives across various languages, you:

* **Overwhelm the Algorithm:** Multiple safe creatives create a statistical buffer
* **Reduce Scrutiny:** The "black creative" is hidden among many approved versions
* **Target Effectively:** Users in your GEO see the correct creative based on their language settings

### Expected Results

✅ **Higher approval rates** for borderline creatives  
✅ **Lower rejection frequency** during ad review  
✅ **Ability to test aggressive angles** with reduced risk
